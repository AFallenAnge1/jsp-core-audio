Why It Exists

Gaming Headsets Were Never
Designed for Comfort

The frustrations are real, consistent, and shared by millions of players. Heavy. Hot. Bulky. The same complaints, every session. JSP Core One started as a response to all of them.

🤕

Head Pressure

Clamping force builds up fast. After a few hours it stops being uncomfortable and starts being genuinely distracting.

🌡️

Heat & Sweat

Closed ear cups trap heat against your skin. Long sessions in any environment eventually become a heat and sweat problem.

👓

Glasses Discomfort

A massive, consistent pain point. Over-ear headsets and glasses simply don't coexist well — and that affects millions of players daily.

📦

Bulk & Weight

Gaming headsets are engineered to sit on a desk stand. For creators and mobile players, portability is an afterthought at best.

😤

Neck & Shoulder Fatigue

You stop noticing the weight mid-session. You notice it when you take the headset off and your neck and shoulders remind you it was there.

🔌

Cable Clutter

Wires that tangle, restrict movement, and wear out at their connectors. A small daily frustration that adds up over every session.

JSP Core One — Design Response
🎧

Comfort-First by Design

No clamping. No heat trap. The physical form factor is being designed around long-session wearability as the primary engineering constraint.

🎒

The Story Behind It

Why JSP Core Audio Exists

"

JSP Core Audio began with a simple frustration: gaming headsets were never designed for comfort during long sessions.

I've been gaming for years, and the same problems kept coming back. The clamp. The heat. The weight that builds up over hours. I'd look at how good earbuds had gotten for everyday listening, and I kept wondering — why doesn't anything like this exist for gaming?

JSP Core One started as a personal answer to that question. A vision to combine immersive gaming audio with the lightweight freedom of creator-grade audio gear. Something portable. Something you can wear all day. Something that doesn't force you to choose between performance and comfort.

It's early. We're in concept and design exploration. But the problem is real — and I believe the right solution can be built.

J
Juan Sotelo Perez Founder · JSP Core Audio
Product Vision

The Vision

JSP Core One is being designed around one idea: gaming audio that feels as good as it sounds. These are the principles guiding every decision we make.

01

All-Day Comfort

Designed to be worn for hours without fatigue, pressure, or heat buildup. Comfort-first isn't a claim — it's the primary constraint shaping the entire hardware direction.

Design Goal
02

Creator-Grade Audio

Intended to serve both gaming and creative workflows. The audio signature is being explored to balance positional gaming clarity with the frequency response creators need for monitoring.

Audio Exploration
03

Low-Profile Setups

Our goal is a complete audio system that disappears into your setup. No bulk, no boom arm, no desk clutter. Minimal hardware footprint for serious audio performance.

Industrial Design Study
04

Modular Flexibility

Each component is designed to work as part of the integrated system — and independently. IEM only. Mic only. Full system. Use what you need.

System Architecture
05

Portable by Default

Planned for real portability — not as a travel mode, but as the standard. Built for players who move between setups and expect their audio to keep up.

Creator Workflow
06

Modern Minimalist Hardware

No aggressive gamer aesthetic. JSP Core One is designed to look as intentional in a studio as it does at a gaming desk — clean, considered, understated.
